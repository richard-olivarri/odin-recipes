THE KRABBY PATTY

The classic krabby patty is definitely the best food item to exist in Spongebob's under-sea world, everyone loves everything about krabby patties.

Here are the ingredients necessary to make this sandwich:

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Sesame seed bun
  • A beef patty made with the secret krabby patty formula
